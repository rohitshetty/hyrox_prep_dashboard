## Key Workout Definitions

### Tempo Runs (T)
- **Pace:** 5:26/km
- **Feel:** Comfortably hard, can speak in short phrases
- **Purpose:** Improves lactate threshold, crucial for sustaining effort between Hyrox stations

### Interval Runs (I)
- **Pace:** 4:58/km
- **Feel:** Hard, breathing heavy, 3-5 word sentences max
- **Purpose:** Builds VO2max, improves ability to recover quickly between efforts

### Easy Runs (E)
- **Pace:** 6:15-6:50/km
- **Feel:** Conversational, relaxed
- **Purpose:** Builds aerobic base, promotes recovery

### Long Runs
- **Pace:** Mostly E pace with M pace segments as prescribed
- **Purpose:** Endurance, mental toughness, Hyrox-specific simulation

### Repetitions (R)
- **Pace:** 4:39/km
- **Feel:** Fast but controlled, focus on form
- **Purpose:** Running economy and speed
